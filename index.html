Voltorb Flip

Difficulty

Status: Normal

Rules

Voltorb Flip is played with a 5x5 grid of tiles and the objective is to flip over all of the high value tiles without flipping over any bombs. Here is a description of the tiles:

Number tiles can have a value of 1, 2, or 3. 2's and 3's are considered high value tiles.

Bomb tiles end your game when you flip them over. It only takes one mistake so be careful.

With the lock button on the bottom right of the board you can lock tiles from being pressed. This can come in handy if you know something is likely a bomb and you do not want to forget.

The tiles on the outside of the board help determine what each tile might be. They contain what the row or column adds up to as well as how many bombs are present. This layout is explained below.

Don't forget that you only need to get all of the 2's and 3's to win. The 1's might help you find out where bombs are, but if you are unsure if a tile is a 1 or a bomb, just leave it!
